# Lived-In Product Photography Frame

## Direct Evidence

The source image combines four independently reusable paradigms:

- diagonal over-shoulder framing
- cropped body-as-context staging
- warm athletic lifestyle grade
- readable UI proof window

## Interpretation

The product is not separated from its use case. The image makes the phone feel like something checked during a real athletic/food-planning moment.

## Aesthetic Role

It balances aspiration with evidence: warmth and proximity create desire; the readable screen creates product clarity.

## Technical Clues

Use the device as a proof object, not the whole frame. Let the human body carry scale, intimacy, and context.

## Reusable Recipe

1. Start with a real routine, not a clean device.
2. Crop close enough that the body becomes environment.
3. Place the phone off-center but readable.
4. Use hard warm light to create texture and energy.
5. Keep the app screen bright enough to explain the product.
