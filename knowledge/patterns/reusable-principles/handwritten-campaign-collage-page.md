# Handwritten Campaign Collage Page

## Direct Evidence

Humanise combines red handwriting, rough marks, and tilted media on a quiet background.

## Interpretation

Use this when the message is about human impact and a polished corporate surface would weaken the argument.

## Reusable Recipe

Limit roughness to memorable touchpoints: nav marks, headings, media rotation, and texture fragments. Keep the underlying reading order simple.
