# Cinematic Product Scene Hero

## Direct Evidence

Lightship places the trailer in a full-bleed landscape scene and overlays the headline directly into that scene.

## Interpretation

This pattern is useful when the product's setting is part of the value proposition.

## Reusable Recipe

Use the scene as the container. Keep chrome sparse. Put text around the product's silhouette, not in a separate card.
