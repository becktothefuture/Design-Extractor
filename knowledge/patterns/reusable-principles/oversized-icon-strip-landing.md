# Oversized Icon Strip Landing

## Direct Evidence

NVG8 presents a black landing viewport with a compact top nav, a massive white headline, one neon CTA, and large colorful tiles that enter from the bottom edge.

## Interpretation

Use this when a product is abstract but can be represented through a small set of memorable symbols. The tiles make the page feel interactive before the user has clicked anything.

## Reusable Recipe

Keep the copy short. Make the icons much larger than normal feature icons. Let the first row crop against the viewport edge, and avoid surrounding them with cards or explanatory text.
