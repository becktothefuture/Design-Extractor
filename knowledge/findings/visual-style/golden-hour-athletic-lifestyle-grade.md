## Interpretation

The image uses food warmth and skin warmth as one system. The phone screen stays neutral-white so the app remains readable.

## Aesthetic Role

It feels appetizing, active, and personal rather than cold, clinical, or studio-polished.

## Technical Clues

Keep the shadows deep enough to create contrast; let sun bands reveal skin texture.

## Reusable Recipe

Use warm direct light and strong shadow shapes when the product lives between performance and appetite.
